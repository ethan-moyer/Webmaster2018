Artificial Intelligence

In Edmund Berkeley’s book “Giant Brains Or Machines That Think” he says “...A machine can handle information; it can calculate, conclude, and choose; it can perform reasonable operations with information. A machine, therefore, can think.” This is the first time that people talked about the idea of a machine thinking and learning. Throughout the 20th century artificial intelligence has been featured in many books and films. Until recently the idea of artificial intelligence was only a dream. Now AI is used by businesses all around the world. AI has saved companies thousands of dollars and changed our day to day lives, even if you realize it or not.
