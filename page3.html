Benefits and Uses

Many people think that AI is bad and can harm your business. Well that is false. AI can help you save money and time. According to survey done by the Sage Group, a company that provide marketing consultants, small companies spend about 120 days a year doing administrative tasks (Sharma). AI can save you time by doing those administrative tasks for you. This means that you can spend more time with your customers and focusing on other things that will help your business. AI is also cost effective because you have to spend less time paying people to do simple administrative tasks and you can pay people who will spend more time with customers and who will come up with better products that can give you more profit.
